We are a premier filming venue located in East Fayette County Georgia, approximately 20 minutes to the East of Peachtree City and about 30 minutes from Atlanta-Hartsfield airport. This property offers 88 acres with wide-open fields, woods with mature hardwood trees, a swamp and a creek. The Grove also has several buildings available for filming. Previous producers have used adjacent property for parking, basecamp, and staging.
